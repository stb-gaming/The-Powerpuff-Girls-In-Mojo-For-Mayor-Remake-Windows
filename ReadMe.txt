What is this?
This is a remake of The Powerpuff Girls in Mojo for Mayor made by SeriousCsaba.

What is The Powerpuff Girls in Mojo for Mayor?
Sky Gamestar was a UK television game provider. It was a service in which Sky TV customers could play interactive games, using a TV remote as a controller. It was shut down on January 31st, 2015. Many games on the platform are now lost media, including The Powerpuff Girls in Mojo for Mayor.
